Reactorcore Art (RC Art)
==
PACK: Motocross Bike

--

Pixel-art sprites pack depicting a modular motocross bike for physics-driven Trials/Elastomania style games. Multiple wheels and connectors to choose from, as well as different color bodies.

--

Drawn with a '32 pixels per meter' scale. Rough estimate, no meticulous accuracy.

--

CC0, you can use these assets however you want, including commercial projects.

--

You can use Alferd Spritesheet Unpacker to quickly split the large atlas image into individual sprites.
https://github.com/ForkandBeard/Alferd-Spritesheet-Unpacker
